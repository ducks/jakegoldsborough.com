Who is Vulfmon

Vulfmon is the alter ego/moniker of Vulfpeck leader Jack Stratton. He sometimes shoots videos in a room with various art on the walls. In the video for Alone Again, Naturally, I noticed a weaving that I loved:

Lucky for me, I just happened to have access to a loom and yarn. Like any new skill, it was a bit awkward to start and honestly, my first one was rough. I stuck with it though and have made many better ones including six inspired by Vulfmon:
